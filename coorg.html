About Coorg

Having peace of intellectual and youth of spirituality, this place is ready to offer whatever it possesses by the grace of God and divine Nature. The dense forests covered with dew of early morning which mist has left behind as a tell-tale sign of her presence, coffee plantations on steep slopes, falling and flowing Cauvery River from a distant place refreshing and nurturing the flora and fauna and its harsh yet soothing noise, a golden yellow-orange ritually spread on the sheet of morning sky acting as backdrop to the contrasting green-brown small-big peaks, and many such vivid sceneries of Nature are a constant companion of those choosing to travel Coorg.

Places To Visit Coorg

Knowing India would ensure one piece of knowledge for sure- go anywhere and there in no chance of missing the sound of temple bells and a sense of belongingness that follows. Add to it the elements of Nature like versicolor fauna amidst equally vivid flora in the ecology of altitude and the picture of Coorg becomes clearer as well as more enchanting. And there is always history of the place to settle down with the missing links. Tour Coorg and see best of this place in its attractions.

  • 1. Omkareshwar Temple

    • Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple is among the oldest in the region built in 1820. While visiting the temple in November during a festival celebrated here, the combination of Gothic and Islamic structure is hard to miss.

  • 2. Padi Igguthappa Temple

    • Built just ten years before Omkaresahwar Temple, its deity is Lord Igguthappa. The holi festival has a different color altogether when celebrated in this premises.

  • 3. Bhangandeshwara Temple

    • Architecture which is prominently found in Kerala could be seen in this temple nestled at convergence of three prime southern rivers, namely, Sujyoti, Cauvery and Kanike at the Brahmagiri Peak.

  • 4. Dargah Sharif of Yemmemadu

    • Bodies of two great souls, Hazrath Sufi Shaeed and Sayyed Hassan Sakaf Halramir rest here making the place worthy of the respect and homage that it receives from people of all faiths.

Frequently Asked Questions about Coorg Tour Packages

Ques. Is 3 days enough for Coorg?

Ans. There are several Coorg packages for three days that give you a decent amount of time to explore the beautiful hill-station. Coorg is a lot about appreciating nature’s wonders and you can cover the following attractions within three days: Madikeri Fort- One of the most famous heritage sites in Coorg. Omkareshwara Temple- An iconic place of worship in the region, popular for its architectural elegance. Gaddige Raja’s Tomb- People visit this tomb for its spectacular location amidst lush greenery and rich history, dating back to 1820. Abbey Falls- Abbey Falls is another tourist-magnet in Coorg, offering a fabulous experience with the gushing waters of the famous Kaveri River. You can also check out the Chiklihole Dam on a 3-day trip to Coorg.


Ques. What is the famous food of Coorg?

Ans. Ensure that your preferred Coorg tour packages include visits to local eateries for sampling the delicious culinary treats of the region. Some of these include the following: Boiled rice and rice gruel which are staple items for the locals. You should not miss trying the Akki Roti or Rice Chapatti which is a delicious breakfast/lunch option. There are several crab, chicken, pork and fish dishes available here. The steam-cooked Puttu is a popular dish here. It has several varieties including Noo, Paa, Kadambuttu, Thaliya, Thambuttu and Nuchu Puttu. Achi Payasa and Pandi Kari (pork curry and rice dumplings) are must-trys in addition to Koli Kari (chicken curry with rice noodles) and Pulav.


Ques. Which is the nearest airport for Coorg?

Ans. Mangalore airport is the closest airport for Coorg, but the second closest airport (Bangalore) offers a larger option of flight connections. Depending on which city you are travelling from, you should check connectivity for both airports


Ques. What is the best time to visit Madikeri?

Ans. Madikeri can be visited all through the year, but the best time is between October to June.
